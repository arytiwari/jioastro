## Classification System

Yogas are classified into 3 tiers based on **importance** and **impact**:

- **TIER 1 - MAJOR**: `importance: "major"` - Life-changing yogas (shown in "Major Positive/Negative Yogas")
- **TIER 2 - MODERATE**: `importance: "moderate"` - Significant impact yogas (shown in "Other Important Yogas")
- **TIER 3 - MINOR**: `importance: "minor"` - Subtle influence yogas (shown in "Additional Combinations")

## TIER 1 - MAJOR YOGAS (~50+ yogas)

### 1. Pancha Mahapurusha Yogas (5)
**Formation:** Benefic planets in kendra (1,4,7,10) in own sign or exaltation

| Yoga | Planet | Effects |
|------|--------|---------|
| Ruchaka | Mars | Courage, leadership, victory over enemies, commander qualities |
| Bhadra | Mercury | Intelligence, eloquence, learning, business acumen |
| Hamsa | Jupiter | Wisdom, righteousness, spiritual knowledge, prosperity |
| Malavya | Venus | Beauty, artistic talents, luxury, marital happiness |
| Sasa | Saturn | Authority, discipline, organizational skills, longevity |

### 2. Sun-Based Yogas (3) ⭐ TIER 1
**Formation:** Planets around the Sun (Soul, Father, Authority)

| Yoga | Formation | Effects |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Vesi | Planet (except Moon) in 2nd from Sun | Wealth, fame, good character, ethical nature, respect |
| Vosi | Planet (except Moon) in 12th from Sun | Skills, authority, independence, self-confidence, leadership |
| Ubhayachari | Planets on both sides of Sun (2nd AND 12th) | Most powerful - combines Vesi and Vosi benefits, wealth, fame, eloquence, balanced nature, royal honors |

**BPHS Reference:** Chapter 34 - Yogadhyaya
**Importance:** Very High - Sun represents soul and authority

### 3. Moon-Based Yogas (4) ⭐ TIER 1
**Formation:** Planets around the Moon (Mind, Mother, Prosperity)

| Yoga | Formation | Effects |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Sunapha | Planet (except Sun) in 2nd from Moon | Self-made wealth, intelligence, prosperity, comfort, policy-making abilities |
| Anapha | Planet (except Sun) in 12th from Moon | Fame, good health, ornaments, enjoyment, renown, well-formed body |
| Durudhura | Planets on both sides of Moon (2nd AND 12th) | Most powerful - royal status, wealth, vehicles, attendants, comforts, balanced mind (combines Sunapha and Anapha) |
| Kemadruma | NO planets in 2nd, 12th from Moon (or conjunct) | **NEGATIVE** - Poverty, struggles, lack of support, mental anguish |

**BPHS Reference:** Chapter 34 - Yogadhyaya
**Importance:** Very High - Moon represents mind and prosperity

### 4. Learning & Wisdom Yogas (2) ⭐ TIER 1

#### Saraswati Yoga ⭐ (One of the MOST auspicious!)
**Formation (BPHS Classical Definition):**
1. Jupiter, Venus, and Mercury ALL in:
   - Kendra houses (1, 4, 7, 10) from Lagna, OR
   - Trikona houses (1, 5, 9) from Lagna, OR
   - 2nd house from Lagna
2. Jupiter must be in strong position:
   - Own sign (Sagittarius, Pisces), OR
   - Exaltation (Cancer), OR
   - Friendly sign (Aries, Cancer, Leo, Scorpio)

**Effects:** Exceptional learning, wisdom, eloquence, artistic talents, mastery of arts and sciences, scholarship, blessed by Goddess Saraswati (deity of knowledge)

**Importance Level:** TIER 1 - MAJOR (Not a wealth yoga!)

#### Budhaditya Yoga
**Formation:** Sun-Mercury conjunction (closer than 14° generally)
**Effects:** Intelligence, wit, communication skills, quick thinking
**Note:** Can be major or moderate based on strength

### 5. Jupiter-Moon Yogas (1)

#### Gaja Kesari Yoga ⭐ (Major!)
**Formation:** Jupiter in kendra (1,4,7,10) from Moon
**Effects:** Wisdom, prosperity, respect, elephants and servants (classical), long life, good character
**Importance:** Very High - Extremely auspicious, one of the most well-known yogas

### 6. Raj Yogas (Multiple)
**Authority & Power Yogas**

| Yoga | Formation | Effects |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Raj Yoga | Kendra lord (1,4,7,10) + Trikona lord (1,5,9) combination | Authority, power, status, leadership, political success |
| Dharma Karmadhipati | 9th lord + 10th lord in relationship | Dharma + career success, righteousness + fame |
| Viparita Raj Yoga | Dusthana lords (6,8,12) in dusthana | Good from bad situations, success after struggles |

### 7. Neecha Bhanga Raj Yoga (4 types)
**Formation:** Cancellation of planetary debilitation

1. Debilitated planet's **dispositor** in kendra from Lagna/Moon
2. Debilitated planet's **exaltation lord** in kendra
3. Debilitated planet **aspected** by its dispositor or exaltation lord
4. Debilitated planet in **kendra** with exaltation lord of that sign

**Effects:** Rags to riches, extraordinary rise after setbacks, success in adversity

### 12. Major Doshas (4)

#### Manglik Dosha (Mars Affliction)
**Formation:** Mars in houses 1, 2, 4, 7, 8, 12 from Lagna, Moon, OR Venus

**Intensity Levels:**
- Very High (7+): 7+ year delays, comprehensive remedies essential
- High (5-7): 5-7 year delays, strong remedial measures needed
- Medium (3-5): 3-5 year delays, remedies recommended
- Low (1.5-3): 2-3 year delays, simple remedies
- Very Low (0-1.5): 1-2 year delays, naturally reduces after 28

**Effects:** Marriage delays/difficulties, partner's health, aggression in relationships

#### Grahan Dosha (Eclipse Affliction)
**Formation:** Sun or Moon conjunct with Rahu or Ketu (within 15°)

**Types:**
- Sun-Rahu (Solar Eclipse): Father issues, ego/authority conflicts
- Sun-Ketu: Spiritual confusion, identity crisis
- Moon-Rahu (Lunar Eclipse): Mental anxiety, mother issues, emotional instability
- Moon-Ketu: Emotional detachment, meditation abilities

**Effects:** Paternal/maternal issues, mental/emotional challenges, health concerns

#### Pitra Dosha (Ancestral Affliction)
**Formation:** 11 indicators with weighted scoring

**Primary Indicators:**
- Sun-Rahu conjunction (paternal lineage karma)
- Moon-Ketu conjunction (maternal lineage karma)
- Rahu in 9th house (ancestral blessing obstacles)
- Ketu in 5th house (progeny issues)

**Effects:** Family lineage issues, progeny difficulties, financial losses, spiritual obstacles

#### Kaal Sarp Dosha
**Same as Kala Sarpa Yoga above** - Can be viewed as major affliction or transformational yoga depending on context

#### Ashraya Group (4) - Based on Sign Types
| Yoga | Pattern | Effects |
|------|---------|---------|
| Rajju | All planets in movable signs (Aries, Cancer, Libra, Capricorn) | Wandering nature, travel, restlessness |
| Musala | All planets in fixed signs (Taurus, Leo, Scorpio, Aquarius) | Stable, stubborn, determined, fixed opinions |
| Nala | All planets in dual signs (Gemini, Virgo, Sagittarius, Pisces) | Adaptable, versatile, changeable nature |
| Maala | Planets distributed across all three types | Balanced personality, multiple talents |

| Yoga | Pattern | Effects |
|------|---------|---------|
| Mala | Benefics and malefics alternating | Mixed results, ups and downs |
| Sarpa | Malefics in kendras (1,4,7,10) | Challenges, obstacles, serpent-like nature |

| Yoga | Pattern | Effects |
|------|---------|---------|
| Yuga | Planets bunched in 4 consecutive houses | Serving nature, helpful |
| Shola | Planets in 6 consecutive houses | Staff/stick pattern, serving others, moderate income |
| Gola | Planets in circular distribution | Rounded personality, balanced |
| Dama | Specific patterns | Controlled nature, restraint |

### 2. Sanyas Yogas (7 Types - Renunciation)
**Classical combinations indicating spiritual inclination or renunciation**

1. Saturn-Venus-Mercury in kendra
2. Four or more planets in one house
3. Moon in navamsa of Saturn/Mars with Saturn in kendra
4. Jupiter in kendra from Moon, Moon in Saturn's sign
5. Lord of 10th house in ascendant, ascendant lord in 10th
6. Sun + Moon in one sign, Saturn in 8th
7. Multiple other classical combinations

**Effects:** Spiritual seeking, renunciation tendencies, detachment, monastic life

## TIER 3 - MINOR YOGAS (144+ yogas)

### 1. Bhava Yogas (144 House Lord Placements)
**Formation:** Each of the 12 house lords can be placed in any of the 12 houses

**Structure:**
- 1st lord in 1st through 12th houses (12 yogas)
- 2nd lord in 1st through 12th houses (12 yogas)
- 3rd lord in 1st through 12th houses (12 yogas)
- ... continuing for all 12 lords ...
- 12th lord in 1st through 12th houses (12 yogas)

**Total:** 12 lords × 12 placements = **144 Bhava Yogas**

**Effects:** Subtle influences based on house lord placement, provides detailed life area insights

### 2. Rare Specialized Yogas

| Yoga | Pattern | Effects |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Matsya | Fish pattern - planets in specific arrangement | Prosperity, spiritual inclinations |
| Kurma | Tortoise pattern - protective distribution | Protection, longevity, stability |
| Kusuma | Flower pattern - beautiful distribution | Aesthetic sense, beauty, arts |
| And other specialized combinations | Various rare patterns | Specific subtle influences |

### 3. Weak/Conditional Yogas
- Any yoga with calculated strength: "Weak"
- Yogas that don't meet major or moderate classification criteria
- Partial yogas or yogas with cancellations
